Joe Dante's lost [assembly cut of *Gremlins*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949430/gremlins-2-5-hour-assembly-cut-just-screened-in-los-angeles-heres-what-we-know/) surfaced in Los Angeles. The rough cut, running 2.5 hours, shows the film before studio intervention. Long rumored and presumed lost, it's the first public screening on record.

Nicolas Winding Refn's *[Her Private Hell](https://www.dreadcentral.com/box-office/570791/neon-dates-refns-her-private-hell-for-july-2026/)* is a slasher. The Cannes 2026 selection hits theaters July 24 via Neon. Details sparse, but a Refn slasher is worth noting.

Curry Barker's untitled *Texas Chainsaw Massacre* reboot [will center the Sawyer family](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/570870/curry-barker-plans-to-explore-the-sawyer-family-in-his-texas-chainsaw-massacre/). Barker's *Obsession* drops next month. His approach to the lineage could distinguish this from the usual legacy-sequel shuffle.

Beau Starr, [Sheriff Meeker in *Halloween 4* and *5*](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/570921/rip-beau-starr-sheriff-meeker-of-halloween-4-5/), has died.

A [*Leslie Vernon* universe short](https://ihorror.com/wait-for-it-a-film-set-in-the-leslie-vernon-universe/) exists: *Wait For It*, written by David J. Stieve (who's also penning *Behind the Mask II*). Scratches the Glen Echo itch while waiting for the sequel.
