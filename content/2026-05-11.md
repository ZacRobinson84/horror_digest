Koji Suzuki, creator of *The Ring*, [died in Tokyo](https://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3950656/the-ring-creator-koji-suzuki-has-passed-away-at-68/) at 68. His 1991 novel spawned the franchise that defined J-horror for a generation.

John Krasinski's *A Quiet Place Part III* [is in production](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3950649/a-quiet-place-part-iii-has-begun-filming-releasing-summer-2027/) with Emily Blunt, Cillian Murphy, and the original cast returning. Summer 2027 release.

Danielle Harris and Scout Taylor-Compton co-wrote and directed *Last Chance Motel*, a slasher [premiering at Cannes](https://www.fangoria.com/last-chance-motel-horror-collective/) this year under The Horror Collective banner.
