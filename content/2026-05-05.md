Perkins and Cage are [reuniting for a Longlegs sequel](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949691/longlegs-2-from-osgood-perkins-and-nicolas-cage-releasing-in-2028/) set for January 2028. Cage produces and stars; Perkins directs. Title TBA.

Daniel Kraus's horror novel [*Angel Down* won the Pulitzer for Fiction](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/571146/angel-down-daniel-kraus-horror-novel-receives-pulitzer-for-best-fiction-book/). Genre recognition at that level is rare enough to matter.

[*Exit 8*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/home-video/3949515/liminal-horror-exit-8-gets-trapped-on-digital-this-week/), the liminal-horror adaptation of the video game, hits digital May 8 via Neon. Psychological claustrophobia in a sterile subway loop.

Egor Abramenko (*Sputnik*) is directing [*Soon You Will Be Gone and Possibly Eaten*](https://www.fangoria.com/dave-franco-alien-thriller-sophie-wilde/), an alien invasion thriller with Dave Franco and Sophie Wilde (*Talk to Me*). Based on a Nick Antosca short story.

Mark Jenkins (*Enys Men*) has [a new film called *Rose of Nevada*](https://www.dreadcentral.com/images/571133/rose-of-nevada-poster-teases-another-eerie-seaside-mystery-from-the-director-of-enys-men/) in the works—another eerie seaside mystery, if the poster's any indication.

Andrew Bowser (*Onyx the Fortuitous and the Talisman of Souls*) is [crowdfunding *Turmoil in the Toybox*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949542/turmoil-in-the-toybox-andrew-bowser-is-crowd-funding-a-nostalgic-new-feature-film/) on Kickstarter. Details sparse, but Bowser's voice alone makes it worth tracking.
