Flanagan wraps production on his *Exorcist* film—neither remake nor sequel, just [his take on the IP](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3955645/mike-flanagans-the-exorcist-has-wrapped-production/). No details yet, but the guy knows how to sustain dread across ten hours, so a two-hour exorcism could land.

Wrong Organ, riding high off *Mouthwashing*, [announced *Carcass Clad*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3955604/mouthwashing-devs-announces-co-op-tank-horror-game-carcass-clad-trailer/)—a three-person co-op tank horror game. War-torn city, mechanical death trap, team communication falling apart under pressure. The studio's track record suggests this won't be a straightforward shooter.

Ryan Murphy's adaptation of Bret Easton Ellis's *The Shards* [premieres August 5 on FX and Hulu](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3955660/ryan-murphys-the-shards-prep-school-serial-killer-series-sets-august-debut/). Ellis's prep school serial killer novel gets the Murphy treatment with cast including Igby Rigney and Homer Gere.

Izabel Pakzad's directorial debut, *Find Your Friends*, [hits Shudder this Friday](https://bloody-disgusting.com/reviews/3955685/find-your-friends-review-slow-burn-revenge-thriller-wants-to-make-you-squirm/). Built from Pakzad's own experience—a menacing car chase during a desert girls' trip. Slow burn, focused on the particular hostility women navigate.

Studiocanal's Sixth Dimension is backing [*Home Safety Hotline*](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/576546/studiocanals-sixth-dimension-boards-creature-feature-home-safety-hotline-from-love-and-monsters-director/), a creature feature from the *Love and Monsters* director. Details sparse.

A 4K restoration of Rainer Werner Fassbinder's *Der Fan* (1982), [that bleak German obsession nightmare](https://rue-morgue.com/german-80s-shocker-der-fan-to-receive-first-u-s-theatrical-release-in-new-4k-restoration/), is getting its first U.S. theatrical run.
