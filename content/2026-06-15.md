Thordur Palsson (The Damned) will [direct the Blumhouse/Atomic Monster Dead by Daylight adaptation](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956122/dead-by-daylight-director-adaptation/), written by Alexandre Aja and David Leslie Johnson-McGoldrick. Announced during the game's 10th anniversary event in Montreal.

Louis Leterrier's [The Last House lands on Netflix this summer](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956125/the-last-house-trailer-wagner-moura-greta-lee-star-in-mysterious-sci-fi-horror-movie-from-netflix/), with Wagner Moura and Greta Lee trapped inside their home with no way out. Trailer is live.

Dead by Daylight's 10th anniversary brings [Art the Clown and Scooby-Doo as playable additions](https://www.fangoria.com/art-the-clown-and-scooby-doo-are-coming-to-dead-by-daylight/), plus new chapters and cosmetics. A film adaptation in the works alongside game updates.

Greg Nicotero joins [Twilight of the Dead](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/576961/greg-nicotero-joins-twilight-of-the-dead-to-bring-romeros-final-zombie-tale-to-life/) as it gears up for summer production. Romero's final zombie script, being brought to life by the makeup legend.

Kevin Cate's viral YouTube short [Open Door gets a feature adaptation](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/576970/open-door-watch-the-viral-youtube-short-being-adapted-into-a-feature/) through a six-figure development deal. Details sparse on scope and timeline.

Ama Lea's [SLASHERCISE assembles horror icons](https://www.fangoria.com/slashercise-movie-bloodstream/) including Felissa Rose and Diana Prince for a film that exists somewhere in the fitness-slasher crossover space.
