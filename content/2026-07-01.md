Parker Finn's [*Possession* remake](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3958010/paul-dano-joins-parker-finns-possession-remake/) adds Paul Dano to its cast. Żuławski's 1981 original still casts a long shadow; details on Dano's role remain sparse.

Yeon Sang-ho (Train to Busan) returns with [*Colony*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3957937/colony-trailer-bites-into-bloody-new-infection-nightmare-from-train-to-busan-filmmaker/), an outbreak film hitting theaters August 28. The trailer suggests something suitably visceral.

Nicolas Winding Refn's [*Her Private Hell*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3957916/her-private-hell-trailer-sends-sophie-thatcher-on-strange-sci-fi-giallo-journey/) arrives July 24 via Neon, with Sophie Thatcher in a sci-fi giallo hybrid that blends the unsettling visual language you'd expect.

Peter Hengl, fresh off *Family Dinner*, has a [Gothic short called *The Fated Hour*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/exclusives/3957753/family-dinner-director-peter-hengl-returns-with-gothic-short-the-fated-hour-exclusive/) hitting festivals this summer with Kier-La Janisse as executive producer. Severin Films will handle distribution.

John Carpenter's audiovisual project *Cathedral* continues rolling out: a [new single, "The Ferryman,"](https://bloody-disgusting.com/music/3957906/john-carpenter-releases-new-single-the-ferryman-from-upcoming-audio-visual-project-cathedral/) precedes the graphic novel's August 4 arrival and companion album on August 7.

Starz is adapting the *Bone Parish* [graphic novel](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3957955/curtis-50-cent-jackson-teams-with-starz-on-supernatural-bone-parish-tv-adaptation/) into a supernatural crime series, with Curtis Jackson producing and Diane Ademu-John and Declan de Barra co-showrunning.

Kino Cult's [*Matinee* Blu-ray](https://bloody-disgusting.com/home-video/3957959/matinee-blu-ray-review-overlooked-canadian-slasher/) revival salvages an overlooked Canadian slasher—theater-set horror that lands harder than most.
