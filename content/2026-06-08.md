[Castlevania: Belmont's Curse](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3955082/castlevania-belmonts-curse-whips-up-october-15-release-for-pc-ps5-and-xbox-trailer/) hits October 15 on PS5, Xbox Series, and PC, with Switch arriving later in 2026. Konami and Evil Empire's whip-em-up finally has a date.

[A Plague Tale: Requiem](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3955069/resonance-a-plague-tale-legacy-releases-august-27-trailer/) (retitled Resonance for this spinoff) lands August 27 on PS5 and Xbox Series with Day One Game Pass. Asobo Studio's rat-infested grimdark continues.

[They Will Come](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3955041/steampunk-horror-adventure-they-will-come-launches-later-this-summer-trailer/), a steampunk horror adventure from Game Pop Studio, is due Summer 2026 on Steam. Details sparse beyond the cinematic premise.

Tribeca brought the festival coverage: [Breeder](https://www.dreadcentral.com/reviews/576008/breeder-tribeca-review/) is Alex Goyette's horror debut mixing gore and dark comedy. [Recluse](https://www.dreadcentral.com/festival-coverage/575945/recluse-tribeca-henry-chaisson-interview/) comes from Henry Chaisson (Antlers), drawing on childhood ghost encounters and classical painters. Both worth a look if you can catch them.

AMC's [Vampire Lestat](https://ihorror.com/never-play-two-nights-in-detroit-amcs-the-vampire-lestat-rewrites-the-rules-in-a-chaotic-season-premiere-review/) premiere ("Detroit") leans into the source material's tonal chaos—a marked shift from Interview with the Vampire's restraint.

[Hold the Fort](https://ihorror.com/horror-comedy-hold-the-fort-arrives-on-digital-june-23/), William Bagley's practical-heavy creature feature, hits digital June 23 after festival runs. Creature effects and gorish comedy in a suburban siege setup.
