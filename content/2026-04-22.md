Curry Barker (Obsession) directing a [new Texas Chainsaw Massacre](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3947450/curry-barker-directing-the-texas-chainsaw-massacre-reboot-movie-for-a24/) for A24, described as a "reimagining." Separate TV series also in development at the studio.

[MORA](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3947426/horror-short-mora-getting-a-feature-film-expansion-from-neon/), Sam Evenson's viral short blending ghosts and AI, getting expanded into a feature by NEON.

Josh Ruben's sci-fi horror Green Bank [starts production this week](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3947415/josh-rubens-horror-movie-green-bank-casts-brittany-ogrady-kumail-nanjiani-and-more/) with Kumail Nanjiani and Brittany O'Grady joining the cast. The creature feature was originally announced years ago.

Panic Fest [announced winners](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/569273/panic-fest-announces-2026-award-winners-obsession-frogman-returns-big-baby-and-more-exclusive/) for its 2026 edition, with Obsession taking a prize alongside entries like Frogman Returns and Big Baby.

Evil Dead Burn shifted to [July 10](https://rue-morgue.com/violent-first-teaser-and-release-date-revealed-for-evil-dead-burn/) from its original July 24 slot. First teaser is online; details sparse but the pitch is straightforward—woman seeks solace with in-laws, Deadites ensue.

[Leviticus](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3947361/leviticus-poster-reveal-queer-horror-movie-trailer-drops-tomorrow/), a queer supernatural horror from NEON, hits theaters June 19. Official trailer drops tomorrow.

Markiplier's [Iron Lung](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3947321/markiplier-will-self-distribute-iron-lung-on-digital-and-physical-media-heres-the-latest-update/) (which hauled $49.9M at the box office) is now getting self-distributed digital and physical releases. Proof indie distribution can still move numbers.
