Kane Parsons' [Backrooms passed $100 million](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954564/backrooms-passing-100-million-in-the-u-s-and-breaking-a-huge-a24-box-office-record/) at the worldwide box office in its opening weekend alone, breaking A24's record for original horror. The film's legs will determine if this stays a fluke or signals actual appetite for high-concept found-footage.

Yeon Sang-ho ([Train to Busan](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954502/colony-train-to-busan-directors-new-zombie-movie-rated-r-for-bloody-violent-content/)) is back with Colony, a zombie film hitting U.S. theaters this summer via Well Go USA. Rated R for bloody violent content.

Adam Wingard is [returning to horror](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/575631/a-new-onslaught-tease-has-the-guest-fans-losing-their-minds/) after years in the studio system. Details are sparse, but [The Guest](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/575631/a-new-onslaught-tease-has-the-guest-fans-losing-their-minds/) fans are already wound up.

Eli Roth's [Ice Cream Man](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954330/eli-roths-ice-cream-man-official-red-trailer-turns-summer-into-a-blood-soaked-massacre/) drops August 7. The red band trailer delivers: kids murdering adults in an idyllic summer town. Co-written by Roth and Noah Belson.

[Fall 2: Deadpoint](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954554/fall-2-deadpoint-fear-of-heights-sequel-arrives-in-theaters-this-september-poster/) arrives September 2. Harriet Slater returns to heights. Trailer tomorrow.

Galder Gaztelu-Urrutia ([The Platform](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954435/rich-flu-trailer-wealth-is-a-death-sentence-in-thriller-from-the-platform-director/)) has [Rich Flu](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954435/rich-flu-trailer-wealth-is-a-death-sentence-in-thriller-from-the-platform-director/) out June 5 via Vertical: a virus that targets the wealthy. Class-conscious body horror.

[Until Dawn 2](https://ihorror.com/until-dawn-2-is-official-and-peter-stormare-is-back/) is official for PS5 in 2027. Peter Stormare returns. Firesprite Games is developing.

Alex Phillips' rust belt giallo [Anything That Moves](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954506/rust-belt-giallo-anything-that-moves-acquired-for-fandor-release-this-july/) lands on Fandor July 14 after festival rounds. Erotic thriller, blood-soaked.
