Markiplier's [*Iron Lung*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3951923/markiplier-horror-movie-iron-lung-comes-home-to-youtube-this-month/) hits YouTube May 31. The self-released theatrical run paid off; now it moves to digital exclusive on his platform.

Na Hong-jin's [creature feature](https://rue-morgue.com/teaser-for-na-hong-jins-cannes-premiering-creature-feature-gives-us-lots-to-hope-for/) drops a teaser ahead of its Cannes premiere. Coming off *The Wailing* and *The Chaser*, the bar's already high.

*Obsession* opened to $15 million and a 96% on RT. Director Curry Barker [already has ideas](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/573002/curry-barker-has-an-idea-for-an-obsession-anthology-series-sequel/) for an anthology and sequel, which is the kind of quick pivot that signals either real momentum or studio panic.

Natalie Erika James's *Saccharine* — supernatural body horror with a candy-coated wrapper — got [an interview](https://rue-morgue.com/interview-candy-and-grime-director-natalie-erika-james-and-the-cast-of-saccharine/) with the director and cast. Details are light in the snippet, but James's track record suggests it's worth attention.

Game demos: BECUZUS released a [rebuilt demo](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3951931/rebuilt-demo-now-available-for-dark-fantasy-rpg-vanran-trailer/) for *VANRAN*, their Souls-like with horror leanings, and a [prologue](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3951921/demo-prologue-now-available-for-psychological-horror-title-dread-eagle-trailer/) for the psychological horror game *Dread Eagle* went live on Steam. Buzzin' Games also announced [*Rock, Paper, Severed*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3951919/play-a-twisted-version-of-a-childhood-classic-with-rock-paper-severed-trailer/), a grimdark take on the hand game, coming later this year.
