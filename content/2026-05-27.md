Yoshihiro Nishimura, the Japanese splatter auteur behind *Tokyo Gore Police*, [has died at 59](https://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3953236/yoshihiro-nishimura-japanese-splatter-master-and-tokyo-gore-police-director-has-passed-away/) after a battle with cirrhosis. His final film, *Geisha War*, an action/horror hybrid, is forthcoming.

Maika Monroe carries *[Victorian Psycho](https://bloody-disgusting.com/reviews/3953278/victorian-psycho-cannes-review-maika-monroe-slays-in-period-horror-comedy/)*, a period horror comedy that premiered at Cannes. The film doesn't pull back on its grotesque kills or its premise—Monroe plays a rage-fueled antiheroine operating in Victorian-era England.

[*Repo! The Genetic Opera*'s creators discuss](https://bloody-disgusting.com/interviews/3953044/repo-the-genetic-operas-creators-on-4k-return-interview/) the film's new 4K restoration and recount a wild production story involving set theft that nearly derailed *The Devil's Carnival*.

Kane Parsons' *[Backrooms](https://www.dreadcentral.com/reviews/574197/backrooms-review-traditional-boiler-room-fuels-safe-results-in-creepypasta-adaptation/)* adaptation lands this weekend. A24's take on the creepypasta opts for traditional horror tropes over stranger territory—it works, safely, but doesn't push.

*[Strange Journey: The Story of Rocky Horror](https://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3953341/celebrate-the-cult-phenomenon-with-strange-journey-the-story-of-rocky-horror-trailer/)*, a documentary from Linus O'Brien (son of *Rocky Horror* creator Richard O'Brien), drops in June with archival footage and new interviews.

[Ti West directs this week's episode](https://www.dreadcentral.com/streaming-home-video/574180/first-look-ti-west-directs-this-weeks-episode-of-apple-tvs-widows-bay/) of Apple TV's *Widow's Bay*, the breakout horror mystery series now halfway through its first season.

Wagner and Jones (the *Dracula* graphic novel team behind *The Impaler*, *The Brides*, and *The Count*) [return with *Dracula: Book IV – The Undead*](https://dailydead.com/the-infamous-vampire-lord-finally-becamethe-undead-matt-wagner-kelley-jones-return-for-dracula-book-iv-the-undead-graphic-novel/), timed for World Dracula Day.
