Capcom's [remaking Code: Veronica](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3954948/capcom-finally-reveals-resident-evil-veronica-coming-in-2027-trailer/) as Resident Evil Veronica, landing 2027 on PC, PS5, and Xbox Series. Details sparse beyond the teaser, but it's happening.

Creative Assembly's [Alien: Isolation 2 is official](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3954951/alien-isolation-2-revealed-bringing-players-planetside-trailer/). Coming to PC, PS5, Xbox Series, and Switch 2, set on Kurasaki Station. The franchise's best game gets a sequel.

Bloober Team announced [SAW: Genesis](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3954954/bloober-team-ready-to-play-a-game-with-saw-genesis-coming-to-early-access/), a 3v1 multiplayer asymmetrical game hitting Early Access. Play as Jigsaw or hunt him on procedurally generated maps set post-WWI.

Jason Blum confirmed [The Blair Witch Project reboot](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954831/the-blair-witch-project-reboot-from-youtube-filmmaker-expected-in-2027/) is targeting 2027, from a YouTube filmmaker. Same window as the new Paranormal Activity.

Anthony Head, who played Giles across 121 episodes of Buffy, [died at 72](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3954886/anthony-stewart-head-buffy-the-vampire-slayer-actor-has-passed-away-at-72/) from pneumonia complications.

[Breeder](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954863/breeder-deranged-poodle-breeder-movie-coming-this-fall-from-shudder-ifc/), about an eccentric poodle breeder luring a broke college student to her remote ranch, premiered at Tribeca and was acquired by Shudder and IFC for fall release.

Kino Lorber picked up [Black Zombie](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954815/black-zombie-kino-lorber-picks-up-documentary-exploring-pre-romero-zombie-cinema/), a documentary from Maya Annik Bedward exploring pre-Romero zombie cinema and its Haitian roots. Theatrical release this fall.
