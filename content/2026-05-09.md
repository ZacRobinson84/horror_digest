Magenta Light Studios acquired [horror-comedy _Lice_](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3950623/horror-comedy-itch-with-emile-hirsch-justin-long-acquired-for-2027-release/) for theatrical release in early 2027, pairing Emile Hirsch and Justin Long in what's pitched as _The Breakfast Club_ meets _Lord of the Flies_. Bleak premise, solid cast pairing.

Brad Caleb Kane's [_Crystal Lake_ prequel](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3950624/crystal-lake-first-look-at-young-jason-voorhees-big-news-coming-this-monday/) is teasing a premiere date announcement for Monday. The showrunner's Instagram post—"News coming. Monday. We're all doomed"—suggests something more than a simple air date.

Mark Heyman, who wrote _Black Swan_, [makes his feature directorial debut with _Pendulum_](https://www.dreadcentral.com/images/571806/pendulum-first-look-features-a-frightened-joseph-gordon-levitt/), produced by Darren Aronofsky. Joseph Gordon-Levitt stars. Release: January 1, 2027 via Vertical Entertainment.

Damien Leone confirmed [_Terrifier 4_ will be set on New Year's Eve](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3950607/terrifier-4-will-be-set-on-new-years-eve/), continuing Art the Clown's holiday slaughter tour after Christmas in part 3.

Toby Harvard (_The Greasy Strangler_, _Come to Daddy_) directs [_Synergy Systems_](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/571826/eric-andre-set-to-save-the-world-in-apocalyptic-action-comedy-synergy-systems/), a punk, near-future action-thriller featuring Eric André, Will Forte, and Tiffany Haddish. His feature debut.

Guillermo del Toro is at work on [_The Buried Giant_](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/571900/guillermo-del-toro-teases-the-buried-giant-as-a-fascinatingly-difficult-stop-motion-movie-for-adults/), which he describes as "a fascinatingly difficult stop-motion movie for adults." Details sparse.

David Hebrero (_Everyone Will Burn_) wrote and directed [_The House of Monsters_](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3950492/money-heists-jaime-lorente-enters-the-house-of-monsters-in-spanish-horror-film/), a Spanish film with Jaime Lorente returning home to care for his ailing father—and whatever else lives there.
