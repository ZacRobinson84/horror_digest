JT Mollner reunites with Aaron Taylor-Johnson, Willa Fitzgerald, and Kyle Gallner for [Skeletons](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3957492/skeletons-willa-fitzgerald-joins-kyle-gallner-in-strange-darling-directors-creature-feature/), a creature feature also starring Brie Larson. Described as a "modern take on the creature horror film" with J.J. Abrams producing—details sparse beyond that.

Robert Eggers' [Werwulf](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3957471/werwulf-official-synopsis-robert-eggers-throwing-modern-werewolf-cliches-out-the-window/) gets its first official image and synopsis. Taylor-Johnson plays a cursed farmer in what Eggers calls his "most visceral and haunting experience yet." The film drops in December.

[Nightborn](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3957403/shudder-acquires-nightborn-for-july-release-date/), Hanna Bergholm's follow-up to *Hatching*, comes to Shudder on July 31 after a Fantasia premiere. A dark fable about parenthood and maternal love, details otherwise sparse.

[Drag](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3957501/dark-comedy-thriller-drag-starring-lizzy-caplan-sets-2027-release/), a dark comedy thriller with Lizzy Caplan and a heist gone wrong in upstate New York, is hitting theaters January 29, 2027. This one played SXSW and landed distribution.

Bryan Fuller's cancelled *Hannibal* trilogy [returns to Netflix](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3957479/hannibal-returns-to-netflix-this-july-with-all-three-seasons-of-bryan-fullers-brilliant-series/) in July—all three seasons, the cliffhanger ending intact.

F.W. Murnau's *Faust* turns 100 this October, and [Mubi is publishing a book](https://bloody-disgusting.com/books/3957407/mubi-to-publish-lost-faust-bible-for-100th-anniversary-of-f-w-murnaus-silent-film-classic/) of production materials previously thought lost. The *Faust Bible* arrives from Mubi Editions and la Cinémathèque française.

Zach Cregger's [*Barbarian*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/home-video/3957412/zach-creggers-barbarian-finally-gets-physical-media-release-this-august/) finally gets physical media on August 11—4K UHD + Blu-ray SteelBook with a new Creepy Duck Design cover.
