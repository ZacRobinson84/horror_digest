Damian McCarthy's [third feature, *Hokum*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/reviews/3940760/hokum-review-sxsw-scary-folkloric-horror/), is in theaters now—a haunted Irish hotel story that lands as his most polished work yet, continuing his streak of unnerving folklore horror after *Oddity* and *Caveat*.

Zach Cregger's [*Resident Evil* teaser](https://rue-morgue.com/new-teaser-trailer-and-photos-from-zach-creggers-resident-evil/) is out. He's treating it as an original story (set during *RE2*'s events in his mind) rather than a game adaptation, with Austin Abrams as a medical courier trapped in a nightmare. Opens September 18.

Joko Anwar has [produced *sMOTHERed*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949076/smothered-trailer-joko-anwar-produced-folk-horror-curses-shudder-in-may/), an Indonesian folk horror film reworking the Malin Kundang legend. Hits Shudder May 29.

Na Hong-Jin (director of *The Wailing*) is premiering [*Hope*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948972/hope-first-images-preview-tense-demilitarized-zone-in-sci-fi-thriller-from-the-wailing-director/) at Cannes next month—a sci-fi thriller set in a village near the DMZ that looks tense and strange.

Ben Leonberg, who made *Good Boy*, is [shooting a serial killer found-footage film called *Follow Mode* entirely on drones](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949027/good-boy-director-to-film-serial-killer-horror-follow-mode-entirely-on-drones/), using consumer drones' automatic tracking feature as the conceit.

The [original *Blair Witch Project* cast and filmmakers](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949029/original-blair-witch-project-filmmakers-stars-to-executive-produce-reboot/) (Sánchez, Myrick, Leonard, C. Williams, Hale) are executive producing Lionsgate's reboot.

[*Atomfall*, the post-apocalyptic folk horror survival game](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3948954/post-apocalyptic-folk-horror-survival-game-atomfall-set-for-tv-adaptation/), is getting adapted for TV by Two Brothers Pictures (*Fleabag* producers).
