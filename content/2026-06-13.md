Spielberg directed [a scene in *Disclosure Day*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/editorials/3955985/steven-spielberg-just-directed-the-scariest-scene-of-his-career-in-disclosure-day/) that the outlet claims is the scariest of his career. Details sparse, but his history with genre—*Poltergeist*, *Duel*, *Jaws*—suggests he knows what he's doing.

[*Resident Evil Veronica*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3955918/capcom-reveals-new-details-for-resident-evil-veronica-will-be-third-person/) is confirmed as third-person. The remake's producer has been talking, but Capcom's reveal trailer kept its cards close.

Isaac Ezban (*Párvulos*, *Evil Eye*) is directing [*Karn Evil 9*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3955940/sci-fi-dystopian-film-karn-evil-9-inspired-by-prog-rock-anthem-in-development/), a sci-fi dystopian film inspired by Emerson, Lake & Palmer's prog anthem, with a script by Tim Hedrick. The hook is unusual enough to watch.

Three films premiered at [Tribeca '26](https://rue-morgue.com/tribeca-26-movie-reviews-mutter-recluse-and-turn-it-up-find-different-kinds-of-familial-fear/): *Mutter*, *Recluse*, and *Turn It Up!* all tackle familial horror from different angles and geography. Worth looking into if your region gets festival overflow.

Greg Nicotero redesigned the [Crypt Keeper puppet for *Tales from the Crypt*](https://www.dreadcentral.com/images/576899/shudder-and-greg-nicotero-bring-new-tales-from-the-crypt-crypt-keepr-puppet-to-life/) now streaming on Shudder. New blood in a classic format.

[*Southern Scares*](https://rue-morgue.com/exclusive-clip-scary-screens-in-southern-scares/) is a found-footage piece rooted in Southern folklore and VHS aesthetics—video-store homage meets local legend hunting.

Peter Sarsgaard joins [*The Last of Us* Season 3](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3955936/peter-sarsgaard-joins-the-last-of-us-season-3-to-play-original-new-character/) as Amon, a new character not in the game—one of The Seraphites' leaders.
