Curry Barker's riding high after Obsession's $300M run—Universal and Blumhouse have locked him for [an original horror film](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956724/curry-barker-writing-and-directing-original-horror-movie-for-universal-blumhouse/), separate from his already-announced Texas Chainsaw Massacre installment and Anything But Ghosts. Details sparse, but the bidding war was real.

Nicolas Winding Refn is reviving [Maniac Cop](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956608/nicolas-winding-refn-shares-early-art-for-his-maniac-cop-franchise-revival/) with concept art already in circulation. A fresh take on the '80s slasher from the guy who made Drive and The Neon Demon—production starts in a few months.

[Her Private Hell](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956651/her-private-hell-gets-a-new-poster-celebrating-the-films-r-rating/), Refn's other genre project, got its R rating for strong/bloody violence, sexual content, and language. Sophie Thatcher stars. The poster's celebrating the rating itself, which is its own kind of statement.

[It Ends](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956601/neons-gen-z-horror-movie-it-ends-heads-to-theaters-this-august/), NEON's feature debut from Alex Ullom, hits theaters August 21. Four recent grads stuck on an endless road. It premiered at SXSW last year to decent noise.

[Hell's Highway: The True Story of Highway Safety Films](https://bloody-disgusting.com/exclusives/3956521/hells-highway-the-true-story-of-highway-safety-films-morbid-shockumentary-gets-2k-restoration-from-kino-cult-trailer-exclusive/) gets a 2K restoration from Kino Cult. A macabre shockumentary about vintage safety films—premieres at Festival Il Cinema Ritrovato in Bologna before hitting US theaters August 7.

Glenn Danzig is [directing an adaptation of his medieval horror comic Hellmask](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/577541/glenn-danzig-directing-adaptation-of-his-medieval-horror-comic-hellmask/). His feature work is rare; this is worth watching.

Rick Baker, the makeup titan behind An American Werewolf in London and basically every major creature effect you care about, will [receive the Vision Award at Locarno this year](https://bloody-disgusting.com/the-further/3956566/makeup-legend-rick-baker-will-receive-the-vision-award-at-this-years-locarno-film-festival/). Seven Oscar wins and still counting.
