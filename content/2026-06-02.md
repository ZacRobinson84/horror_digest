Zack Snyder is [remaking Escape from New York](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954177/escape-from-new-york-zack-snyder-directing-remake-of-john-carpenter-classic/), writing and directing his take on Carpenter's 1981 dystopia. After Dawn of the Dead, he's moving on to another genre master's work.

Na Hong-jin's creature feature [Hope arrives September 9](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954149/hope-korean-creature-feature-coming-to-u-s-theaters-in-september/) via NEON. The film stars Hwang Jung-min, Hoyeon, Taylor Russell, and Michael Fassbender alongside Alicia Vikander and Cameron Britton—a genuinely unexpected ensemble for a Korean monster movie.

David Robert Mitchell's [The End of Oak Street](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954025/the-end-of-oak-street-official-trailer-a-cosmic-event-unleashes-dinosaurs-in-suburbia/) drops August 14. A cosmic event unleashes dinosaurs in suburbia. Details sparse but the premise is enough.

[Backrooms](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954023/backrooms-just-had-the-highest-opening-weekend-for-an-original-horror-movie-in-history/) just had the highest opening weekend for an original horror film in history. Kane Parsons, a 20-year-old YouTuber who built a following on liminal space videos, directed it for A24—also breaking the distributor's all-time opening record.

Gary Dauberman's Coin Operated picked up [Yellowjacket Summer](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954101/yellowjacket-summer-robert-mccammon-horror-story-getting-movie-adaptation/), a Robert McCammon creature feature. Daniel Kraus (Whalefall) is scripting.

[Hokum](https://bloody-disgusting.com/home-video/3954152/hokum-heads-home-to-digital-tomorrow-ahead-of-physical-media-release-in-august/) (Damian McCarthy's follow-up to Oddity) hits digital June 2, then physical August 11. Adam Scott stars.

Rue Morgue has an [exclusive set visit](https://rue-morgue.com/exclusive-set-visit-frenzy-moon-a-practical-effects-monster-movie-with-heart/) to Gregory Lamberson's Frenzy Moon, a practical effects werewolf film.
