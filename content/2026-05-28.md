Grindhouse Releasing is [bringing *Cannibal Holocaust* to theaters in 4K](https://www.fangoria.com/cannibal-holocaust-grindhouse-releasing-theaters/)—Deodato's notorious 1980 feature in eye-watering restoration. It's a rare theatrical run for one of horror's most deliberately difficult films.

Jane Schoenbrun's *[Teenage Sex and Death at Camp Miasma](https://www.fangoria.com/teenage-sex-and-death-at-camp-miasma-trailer/)* opens in August. A young director is hired to resurrect a defunct slasher franchise, only to spiral into a "blood-soaked world of desire, fear, and delirium" when she meets the reclusive original star. Premiered at Cannes Un Certain Regard to the kind of reviews that suggest critics lost their minds in the best way.

Knifepoint Horror, the cult podcast, is becoming a feature with *[Fiona](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3953463/fiona-maggie-grace-starring-in-horror-movie-based-on-knifepoint-horror-podcast/)*—Maggie Grace and Steve Howey starring in what's described as a witch story about a lonely divorced doctor. Details sparse but Capstone Studios (Obsession, Don't Move) is producing.

Turkish creature feature *[Mutter: The Diary of a Mother](https://bloody-disgusting.com/exclusives/3953322/mutter-the-diary-of-a-mother-exclusive-teaser-gives-birth-to-a-gnarly-new-creature-feature/)* hits Tribeca next weekend. A woman gives birth to something inhuman under traumatic circumstances. The teaser suggests this goes places most pregnancy horror won't follow.

Donald Farmer (producer, cult exploitation fixture) is back with *[WereShark](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3953397/wereshark-official-trailer-this-shark-has-legs-and-barks-at-the-moon/)*—a no-budget creature hybrid that does exactly what the title promises. August 25.

Indonesian survival-slasher *[AGNI: Village of Calamity](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3953355/wired-productions-to-publish-indonesian-survival-horror-game-agni-village-of-calamity-trailer/)* signed with Wired Productions for worldwide distribution. Coming to PC, Xbox Series, and PlayStation 5.
