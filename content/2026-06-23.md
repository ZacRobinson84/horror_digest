Dan Trachtenberg is directing [Freddy the 13th](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956882/freddy-the-13th-dan-trachtenberg-directing-animated-horror-comedy-slasher-for-paramount/), an animated horror-comedy for Paramount that imagines Uncle Buck as a slasher villain. Based on a graphic novel by Yehudi Mercado.

Ken Russell's [The Devils](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956815/ken-russells-controversial-the-devils-restoration-gets-first-teaser-ahead-of-theatrical-release/) restoration is hitting theaters this fall uncut, courtesy of Warner Bros. Clockwork. First official teaser is up; the holy grail restoration finally materializes after 50 years.

Drew Hancock ([Companion](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956968/seasons-lily-james-starring-in-reddit-horror-movie-from-companion-director-drew-hancock/)) is directing Seasons for Amazon MGM Studios with Lily James starring. Adapted from a viral Reddit short story about a couple who buy haunted land.

Robert Eggers' [Werwulf](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956833/werwulf-focus-features-chairman-promises-a-robert-eggers-horror-movie-on-a-whole-other-level/) is shaping up to be something else entirely, according to Focus Features chairman Peter Kujawski, who called it "on a whole other level." Trailer incoming.

Andy Muschietti is producing [Exposure](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/577784/warner-bros-grabs-psychosexual-thriller-exposure-with-andy-muschietti-producing/), a psychosexual thriller Warner Bros. just acquired. Details sparse on the project itself.

Catherine Corcoran ([Terrifier](https://www.fangoria.com/catherine-corcoran-raindance-film-festival/)) is heading up a new horror initiative at Raindance Film Festival. No details yet on what that entails.

Mark Jenkin's [Rose of Nevada](https://www.fangoria.com/george-mackay-gets-haunted-in-exclusive-rose-of-nevada-clip/) is now in select theaters—a Cornish ghost story starring George MacKay. Exclusive clip available.
