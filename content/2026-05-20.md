Creepypasta-inspired [The Summoning](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3952378/the-summoning-trailer-creepypasta-anthology-brings-urban-legend-to-life/) hits VOD June 5—three teens summon Baby Blue on a dare, survival optional. The indie film leans on the urban legend mechanics that actually work in short form, though details sparse on how it stretches to feature length.

Stallone's Balboa Productions is developing a [serial killer series](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3952299/sylvester-stallone-the-walking-dead-vet-developing-4mk-series-based-on-serial-killer-novels/) from J.D. Barker's 4MK novels with Walking Dead showrunner Channing Powell attached. No timeline yet, but the pairing suggests they're aiming for something sharper than standard prestige-TV sadism.

Sean Byrne (Dangerous Animals) is directing [The Mannequin](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3952261/lili-reinhart-joins-serial-killer-thriller-the-mannequin-from-dangerous-animals-director/), a serial killer thriller with Lili Reinhart and Melissa Leo. Plot locked down, but Byrne's track record with controlled dread suggests this won't be another by-the-numbers procedural.

[The Devil Queen](https://bloody-disgusting.com/exclusives/3952215/the-devil-queen-john-waters-meets-martin-scorcese-in-4k-restoration-of-rare-cult-gem-exclusive-trailer/) — a transgressive '70s Brazilian crime film loosely inspired by legendary gangster João Francisco dos Santos — is getting a Kino Lorber 4K restoration next month. Waters meets Scorsese, camp bleeding into genuine menace, actually worth the restoration hype.

Ian McCulloch (Lucio Fulci's Zombie) returns after a decade-plus absence for [Chronicles of Depression](https://bloody-disgusting.com/exclusives/3952086/chronicles-of-depression-teaser-trailer-zombies-ian-mcculloch-returns-in-psychological-horror-film-exclusive/), a psychological horror set in a 1950s British institution. Watch for the Shadow Man. His face still carries weight.

John Carpenter is releasing a [metal album](https://www.fangoria.com/john-carpenter-new-metal-album-cathedral/) called Cathedral in August on Sacred Bones, paired with a graphic novel from Storm King. First single "Lord of the Underground" is out now. He's still hungry.

Andrew Bowser (Onyx the Fortuitous) is [crowdfunding](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949542/turmoil-in-the-toybox-andrew-bowser-is-crowd-funding-a-nostalgic-new-feature-film/) Turmoil in the Toybox on Kickstarter — a nostalgic feature rooted in a 1984 find. His projects tend to reward attention.
