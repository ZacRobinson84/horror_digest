Radio Silence directing [The Mummy 4](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948047/radio-silences-the-mummy-4-starring-brendan-fraser-rachel-weisz-set-for-halloween-2027/) with Fraser and Weisz back—moved up to October 2027. That's the news.

Parker Finn remaking [Żuławski's Possession](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948010/possession-remake-from-parker-finn-will-star-margaret-qualley-and-callum-turner/) with Margaret Qualley and Callum Turner. Finn's still riding Smile's momentum into a project that could either be pointless or genuinely unhinged depending on how he handles it.

Dutch folk horror [Heresy](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948000/heresy-trailer-dutch-folk-horror-film-streams-on-shudder-in-may/) (also Witte Wieven) hits Shudder May 1. Medieval village, faith, witchcraft—the setup's solid.

Shudder's May slate finally brings [Tales from the Crypt](https://bloody-disgusting.com/home-video/3948004/shudder-may-programming-includes-tales-from-the-crypt-whistle-livid-in-search-of-darkness/) to streaming for the first time, plus exclusives Whistle and This Is Not a Test. The Crypt thing alone justifies checking the schedule.

Noah Hawley's prepping [Alien: Earth season 2](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3947994/alien-earth-creator-noah-hawley-talks-season-2-beyond-we-could-go-for-as-long-as-we-want/) to shoot this summer. Hawley's already thinking past season 3.

Grady Hendrix [revived his haunted house podcast](https://bloody-disgusting.com/podcasts/3947948/grady-hendrix-explores-haunted-house-history-with-super-scary-haunted-homeschool-podcast/) for season two with Chris LaMartina (WNUF Halloween Special) as co-host. Worth revisiting if you missed the first run.

Indie slasher [Frankie, Maniac Woman](https://www.fangoria.com/frankie-maniac-woman-new-horror-movie-slasher/) is in select theaters now—grindhouse energy, deserves the grassroots push according to those who've caught it.
