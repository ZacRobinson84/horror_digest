Bloober Team's on a roll: after [SAW: Genesis](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3955034/bloober-returns-to-star-trek-with-psychological-thriller-star-trek-shadow-frontier-trailer/), they've announced *Star Trek: Shadow Frontier*, a psychological thriller developed with Paramount Games. Second Trek title for them after the VR body horror game.

[*The Sinking City 2*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3955008/the-sinking-city-2-rises-to-august-18-release-date-demo-shadow-dropped-trailer/) hits August 18. Frogwares shadow-dropped a demo during Future Games Show—playable opening hour available now.

[*Mutter: The Diary of a Mother*](https://www.dreadcentral.com/reviews/574844/mutter-the-diary-of-a-mother-tribeca-2026-review/), a subversive creature feature out of Tribeca this year, is worth attention. Details sparse but the festival track suggests something worth watching.

Studio Abattoir's [*The Pines*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3955028/psychological-horror-title-the-pines-slated-for-a-2027-release-trailer/)—a psychological horror about a therapy retreat hosting unstable residents and dangerous conspiracies—is slated for 2027.

First clip from Sébastien Vaniček's [*Evil Dead Burn*](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/575934/first-clip-from-evil-dead-burn-drinks-hot-wax-to-the-fullest/) dropped. The wax detail suggests the practical excess fans want.

[Killer Shorts](https://bloody-disgusting.com/the-further/3954902/killer-shorts-horror-competition-open-now-with-massive-expansion-into-feature-screenplays/) expanded beyond shorts this year—now accepting feature screenplays. Competition's been a solid launchpad since 2019.
