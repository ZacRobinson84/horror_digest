Ernest Dickerson (Demon Knight) is directing Black Diamond, an adaptation of the folk horror comic for Fangoria Studios. The film follows a family ski trip that spirals into something darker, with original creator Brendan Columbus writing the screenplay.

Michael Sarnoski's [The Death of Robin Hood](https://bloody-disgusting.com/interviews/3956368/the-death-of-robin-hood-director-michael-sarnoski-interview/) reimagines the folk legend as a grizzled, violent reckoning with legacy. A24 releases it Friday with Hugh Jackman in the lead.

Stephen Graham Jones has a new haunted house novella, Ears, [coming out](https://bloody-disgusting.com/exclusives/3956287/stephen-graham-jones-haunted-house-novella-ears-exclusive-cover-reveal/) — the prolific author continues mining domestic dread.

Rod Blackhurst (Dolly) has found footage horror Hedges in the works, [teaser dropping](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956318/hedges-teaser-uncovers-found-footage-frights-from-dolly-director/) this week. A man secretly films his wife losing her grip on reality, cameras catching something worse.

Kari Skogland (The Handmaid's Tale) is directing Lady Macbeth, an adaptation of Ava Reid's gothic reimagining of Shakespeare's villain, with Emma Donoghue writing. Feminist reworking of the source material.

Yellow Veil Pictures acquired Corpus, a nightlife thriller premiering at Fantasia. Details sparse, but the festival lineup suggests something worth tracking.

Lucile Hadžihalilović's films are being [collected for the first time on North American Blu-ray](https://ihorror.com/the-worlds-of-lucile-hadzihalilovic-collected-at-last/), spanning four films in one box set. The director discussed the box in a recent interview.
