Rachel Kempf and Nick Toti return with [*Homebody*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949946/homebody-trailer-lo-fi-ghost-story-shot-on-minidv-tape-transferred-to-vhs/), a lo-fi ghost story shot on MiniDV and transferred to VHS. After refusing to release their previous film on streaming or physical media, they're doubling down on the analog bit.

[Del Toro supervised a 4K restoration of *Pan's Labyrinth*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949918/pans-labyrinth-ken-russells-the-devils-restorations-to-screen-at-cannes/) ahead of its Cannes Classic screening on May 12. Ken Russell's *The Devils* is also getting restored for the festival—the uncut version has been a white whale for decades.

Greg McLean's back to horror with [*The First Exorcist*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949892/wolf-creek-directors-biblical-horror-the-first-exorcist-possesses-josh-heuston-ryan-corr/), a biblical horror film drawing from the Gospels. Josh Heuston and Ryan Corr star. His first feature in this space since *Wolf Creek*.

Neon picked up [*King Snake*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949825/jeff-nichols-gothic-horror-king-snake-starring-margaret-qualley-acquired-by-neon/), Jeff Nichols' Southern gothic with Margaret Qualley and Drew Starkey as a couple who inherit a cursed Arkansas farm. Nichols' first horror feature.

[*Evil Dead Burn*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949808/evil-dead-burn-official-trailer-the-most-grueling-family-reunion-of-all-time/) trailer is live—the sixth entry looks genuinely vicious. Hits July 10.

Fantasia's [30th edition announced its first wave](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949639/fantasia-2026-first-wave-includes-the-last-temptation-of-becky-hot-spot-steve-johnson-doc/), including *The Last Temptation of Becky* with Neil Patrick Harris, *Ferine* with Carolyn Bracken, and a doc on practical effects artist Steve Johnson (July 16–Aug 2).

Jonathan Tiersten, best known for the original [*Sleepaway Camp*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3949742/original-sleepaway-camp-star-jonathan-tiersten-has-passed-away-at-60/), has died at 60.

Darren Lynn Bousman and Paris Hilton are making [*Road to Reality*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949711/road-to-reality-paris-hilton-and-darren-bousman-join-forces-for-reality-show-horror-movie/), a horror film set in a collapsing reality TV franchise. Their first collaboration since *Repo! The Genetic Opera*.
