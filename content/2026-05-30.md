Damien Leone's next after the Terrifier run is [an original for Lionsgate](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3953869/tortures-of-the-damned-damien-leone-directing-original-horror-movie-for-lionsgate-ghost-house/), Tortures of the Damned, which he'll write, direct, and produce with Sam Raimi on board as a producer.

David Robert Mitchell (It Follows) returns August 14 with [The End of Oak Street](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3953842/the-end-of-oak-street-new-images-unleash-unique-dinosaur-movie-from-it-follows-director/), a sci-fi thriller starring Anne Hathaway and Ewan McGregor. Details sparse, but J.J. Abrams is producing and there's a dinosaur involved.

Tom Spezialy (The Leftovers, Watchmen) is adapting [The Birds for a limited series](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3953947/the-birds-sarah-snook-attached-to-limited-series-remake-of-nature-gone-wild-classic/) with Sarah Snook attached to star. Being shopped to networks now.

Jim Mickle (Stake Land) and E.L. Katz are developing [That Texas Blood](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3953909/that-texas-blood-cormac-mccarthy-meets-john-carpenter-in-fxs-neo-noir-tv-series/) for FX, adapting the Image Comics series—described as "Cormac McCarthy meets John Carpenter."

Yeon Sang-ho's [Colony premiered at Cannes](https://www.dreadcentral.com/reviews/574710/colony-ferocious-terrifying-and-a-new-zombie-classic-cannes-2026-review/) and one reviewer's calling it "Train to Busan on steroids" and the best zombie film in years.

A [lost episode of Late Night Horror](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3953828/lost-late-night-horror-episode-debuts-this-september/), a vampire anthology, surfaced and debuts this fall. Details sparse.

Adam Wingard's (The Guest) action-horror [Onslaught is coming September](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3953792/the-guest-director-adam-wingards-onslaught-gets-a-september-theatrical-release-date/). Trailer playing in theaters now, no images online yet.
