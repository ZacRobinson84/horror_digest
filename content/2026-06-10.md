Daniel Stamm returns with [Lockbox](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3955476/lockbox-trailer-carla-gugino-lou-taylor-pucci-face-inhuman-entity-in-new-horror-from-the-last-exorcism-director/), adapting a Knifepoint Horror story with Carla Gugino and Lou Taylor Pucci facing down an entity summoned from an grimoire. Hits theaters July 3.

David Robert Mitchell's [They Follow](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3955521/they-follow-naomi-ackie-joining-maika-monroe-in-it-follows-sequel/) sequel reunites him with Maika Monroe and adds Naomi Ackie (Blink Twice) in final negotiations. Plot details remain sparse.

[Whalefall](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3955401/whalefall-teaser-trailer-austin-abrams-is-literally-buried-alive-inside-a-whale-this-october/) drops a genuinely unsettling teaser: Austin Abrams swallowed alive by a whale, fighting for survival in the thing's gullet. October 16 theatrical release.

Joe Begos's [Jimmy and Stiggs](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3955489/jimmy-and-stiggs-joe-begos-horror-movie-getting-an-argentine-spinoff-movie/) — the neon splatterfest from The Horror Section — is spawning an Argentine spinoff directed by Sebastián De Caro, who acquired local adaptation rights.

[Gonjiam: Haunted Asylum](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3955423/gonjiam-haunted-asylum-gets-indonesian-horror-remake-korean-haunted-hospital/) gets an Indonesian found-footage remake titled Korean Haunted Hospital (402: Rumah Sakit Angker Korea), directed by Anggy Umbara. World premiere next month before Indonesian theatrical release.

Gabe Bartalos and Bill Plympton collaborating on [Highway Gothic](https://rue-morgue.com/exclusive-art-comments-gabe-bartalos-and-bill-plympton-are-creating-a-new-slasher-for-highway-gothic/), a live-action/animation hybrid slasher. Surreal premise, practical effects from Bartalos, distinctive animation from Plympton.

Marina de Van's body-horror films — [In My Skin](https://bloody-disgusting.com/editorials/3955454/in-my-skin-and-dark-touch-marina-de-van-horror-movies/) and Dark Touch — get a [deep-dive essay](https://bloody-disgusting.com/editorials/3955454/in-my-skin-and-dark-touch-marina-de-van-horror-movies/) on her use of self-destruction as metaphor for trauma, cementing her place in New French Extremity.
