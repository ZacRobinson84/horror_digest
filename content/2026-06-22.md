Bobby Prince, composer for [Wolfenstein 3D and Doom](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3956777/wolfenstein-3d-doom-composer-bobby-prince-dies-at-81/), has died at 81. His work defined the sound of early id Software games.

AMC's The Vampire Lestat is two episodes in and [finding its footing](https://ihorror.com/the-toronto-skyline-is-something-to-behold-the-vampire-lestat-finds-its-emotional-center-in-sam-reid-and-jacob-andersons-performances-review/) with Reid and Anderson's chemistry anchoring the messier elements. The show is leaning harder into sex and absurdity than its predecessor.

Round the Decay, a [folk horror creature feature](https://ihorror.com/round-the-decay-lets-the-wrexsoul-out/) from Adam Newman, sends hikers into a town with a tomb nobody talks about. Details sparse on the Wrexsoul itself, but the setup lands.

Lethal Wedding, the Zombies Ate My Neighbors tribute from Mega Cat Studios, [cleared its Kickstarter goal by 150%](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3956775/mega-cat-studios-releases-new-dev-vlog-for-zombies-ate-my-neighbors-send-up-lethal-wedding-watch/) and a new dev vlog shows the team walking through the work.

Camp, a [lo-fi coming-of-age film](https://www.dreadcentral.com/reviews/577233/camps-quiet-queer-magic-enraptures-chattanooga-review/) with supernatural undertones, played Chattanooga and found an audience for its quiet approach to queer magic.
