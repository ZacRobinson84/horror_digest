DC's [Clayface](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3947482/clayface-teaser-trailer-dc-unleashes-a-full-on-body-horror-nightmare-this-halloween/) arrives October 23 as James Gunn's horror play—body horror in the Cronenberg vein, with Tom Rhys Harries as the Hollywood-to-monster descent. The teaser lands today.

Magnolia Pictures grabbed North American rights to [Never After Dark](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3947597/japanese-haunter-never-after-dark-acquired-for-fall-release/), a Japanese haunter from Dave Boyle about a medium's exorcism gone wrong in the countryside. Fall theatrical release.

Joko Anwar's [Satan's Slaves 3: Origin](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3947552/joko-anwars-satans-slaves-3-origin-is-officially-on-the-way-next-year/) is officially in the works for next year. Word came via a teaser that played ahead of his current film Ghost in the Cell in Indonesian theaters.

Maika Monroe leads [Victorian Psycho](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3947600/maika-monroe-embraces-killer-instincts-in-gothic-victorian-psycho-first-image/), a Zachary Wigon psychological thriller premiering at Cannes before its September 25 theatrical release. She plays the killer this time.

Natalie Erika James (Relic) sends up diet culture into body horror with [Saccharine](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3947496/diet-culture-leads-to-haunting-body-horror-in-natalie-erika-james-saccharine-trailer-exclusive/), where a weight-loss craze involving human ashes opens a haunting world of hurt. May 22 via Shudder and IFC.

AMC's [The Terror: Devil in Silver](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3947593/preview-the-atmospheric-opening-scene-of-amcs-the-terror-devil-in-silver/) returns May 7 with Dan Stevens in a psychiatric hospital. An opening scene preview is out.

A Lovecraftian fishing game called [The Wake](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3947476/steam-demo-launching-april-24-for-lovecraftian-fishing-game-the-wake-trailer/) gets a Steam demo April 24 from Playground Pigeon.
