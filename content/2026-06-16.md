Romero's lost *Day of the Dead* elements have surfaced. Scream Factory unearthed the original interpositive and is releasing [a newly restored 4K edition](https://bloody-disgusting.com/interviews/3955499/day-of-the-dead-4k-restoration-interview/) with a four-disc set loaded with special features and new interviews.

Greg Nicotero is joining the makeup effects team for [*Twilight of the Dead*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956167/twilight-of-the-dead-greg-nicotero-joins-the-makeup-effects-team-for-romero-zombie-movie/), the Paz Brothers' adaptation based on Romero's own treatment, starring Kate Beckinsale.

Thordur Palsson (*The Damned*) will [direct Blumhouse's *Dead by Daylight* adaptation](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956122/dead-by-daylight-director-adaptation/), announced during the game's 10th anniversary event.

Louis Leterrier's [*The Last House*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956125/the-last-house-trailer-wagner-moura-greta-lee-star-in-mysterious-sci-fi-horror-movie-from-netflix/) arrives on Netflix this summer with Wagner Moura and Greta Lee. A family sealed inside their home with no way out—details sparse beyond that.

[Rod Blackhurst's *Alone Time*](https://www.fangoria.com/alone-time-short-film-movie-adaptation/), a viral short, is getting a feature expansion. Another YouTube-to-theater pipeline story following *Obsession* and *Backrooms*.

Curry Barker is [directing a new *Texas Chain Saw Massacre* film](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956142/curry-barkers-the-texas-chainsaw-massacre-movie-aims-to-capture-the-rawness-of-the-original-classic/) for New Line, aiming to recapture Hooper's rawness. This comes after *Obsession* crossed $300 million worldwide.

*Hellraiser: Revival* launches October 6 for PC, PS5, and Xbox Series, available in standard and deluxe editions with pre-orders [live now](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3956242/hellraiser-revival-has-such-sights-to-show-you-when-it-launches-on-october-6-trailer/).
