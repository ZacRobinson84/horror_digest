Until Dawn 2 is [heading tropical](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3954400/developed-by-firesprite-games-until-dawn-2-revealed-coming-in-2027-trailer/) instead of back to Blackwood Mountain. Firesprite's handling it, arriving 2027 on PS5.

Michael Sarnoski is [directing the pilot](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3954315/the-chain-michael-sarnoski-directing-pilot-episode-for-disturbing-hbo-kidnapping-thriller/) for HBO's The Chain, Adrian McKinty's twisted novel about kidnapping your neighbor's child to get your own back. Stephen King's a fan.

Kane Parsons is [hunting a screenwriter](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954256/backrooms-2-kane-parsons-reportedly-looking-for-screenwriter-to-collaborate-with/) for Backrooms 2. The first one cleared nine figures for A24, so this was inevitable.
