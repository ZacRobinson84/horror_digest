Halsey co-writes and stars in [Replacer](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3951494/halsey-will-star-in-psychosexual-horror-movie-replacer-plot-details-revealed/), a psychosexual horror from director Avan Jogia with Lilly Wachowski executive producing. Details are sparse beyond the Montreal setting, but the pairing of Jogia with Wachowski's involvement suggests something more interesting than standard genre fare.

Pascal Laugier (Martyrs, Incident in a Ghostland) is directing [Nocturnal](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3951253/noctural-martyrs-filmmaker-pascal-laugier-directing-female-vampire-horror-movie/), a female vampire film from a screenplay by David Birke. Shooting targeted for fall in Latvia.

Jane Schoenbrun's [Teenage Sex and Death at Camp Miasma](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/572627/jane-schoenbruns-insanely-over-the-top-slasher-teenage-sex-death-at-camp-miasma-gets-6-minute-standing-ovation-at-cannes/) got a six-minute standing ovation at Cannes—an '80s slasher homage that apparently earns its excess, rated R for bloody violence and gore.

The [Scary Movie](https://www.dreadcentral.com/video/572633/the-gang-is-back-in-new-scary-movie-teaser-watch/) franchise is back with a sixth entry, leaning hard into '90s/early-aughts nostalgia.

Eduardo Sanchez and Gregg Hale (Blair Witch Project) are producing [The Influencer Project](https://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3951306/blair-witch-project-creators-to-produce-found-footage-horror-the-influencer-project/), a found-footage film starring British influencer Chiara King as a target of a stalker. Shot in Copenhagen, based on true events.
