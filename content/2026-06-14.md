Greg Nicotero is joining [Twilight of the Dead](https://dailydead.com/exclusive-greg-nicotero-joins-twilight-of-the-dead/), the long-gestating Romero sequel. He's at Living Dead Weekend in Monroeville, where Dawn of the Dead was shot—details sparse on what his involvement entails, but the timing suggests movement on a project that's been in development hell for years.

Dread Central published [a retrospective on Ed and Lorraine Warren](https://www.dreadcentral.com/editorials/573685/the-true-story-of-the-warrens-and-annabelle/) and their actual history versus the mythology built around them. Worth reading if you've been watching the Conjuring films and want to know what's real and what's Hollywood invention.
