Ari Aster [wrote a prequel to *Hereditary*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3955357/ari-aster-reveals-that-he-wrote-a-prequel-to-hereditary/), though he's noncommittal about whether it'll ever get made. Eight years after his debut reshaped modern horror, he's apparently been thinking about the mythology long enough to script what comes before.

Netflix's *Human Vapor* adaptation [drops July 2](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3955368/human-vapor-official-trailer-classic-toho-film-gets-a-netflix-reboot-series-this-summer/), turning Ishirō Honda's 1960 tokusatsu film into an eight-episode series. Shun Oguri plays a suspended detective pulled into a case involving invisibility and murder.

Lauren LaVera and Rory Culkin are [starring in *Red Wedding*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3955291/lauren-lavera-and-rory-culkin-starring-in-josh-stolberg-horror-movie-red-wedding/), Josh Stolberg's home invasion film following a teenager and stepmother kidnapped by a cult. Stolberg wrote *Saw X* and *Piranha 3D*.

*Backrooms* [crossed $200 million worldwide](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3955261/backrooms-just-passed-200-million-at-the-worldwide-box-office/), the A24 liminal horror breakout now firmly a cultural moment. Second weekend hold was solid at $25.9M domestic.

A [developer presentation for *When Sirens Fall Silent*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3955101/lka-delves-deeper-into-the-horror-of-when-sirens-fall-silent-in-new-presentation-video/) showcased the psychological horror game's world and cast. LKA and Wired Productions released three minutes of narrative-focused footage during Summer Game Fest.

Cynthia Pelayo's *It Came From Neverland*—[a dark reimagining of Peter Pan](https://bloody-disgusting.com/books/3955287/it-came-from-neverland-review-a-stunning-devastating-take-on-peter-pan/)—layers myth and body horror into something genuinely unsettling. The review notes Pelayo's gift for finding dread in shared cultural stories.

Two Tribeca entries worth noting: *Dante*, [a paramedic's night shift spiraling into carnage](https://bloody-disgusting.com/reviews/3955047/dante-review-a-paramedics-night-shift-turns-into-a-blood-soaked-nightmare-tribeca-2026/), and [*Breeder*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/reviews/3955073/breeder-review-tribeca-2026/), Alex Goyette's feature debut using eugenics as the foundation for something genuinely provocative.
