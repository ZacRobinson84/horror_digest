Tim Fehlbaum (September 5) will [direct an English-language remake](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948534/french-mystery-thriller-black-box-set-for-english-remake-from-september-5-director/) of the French thriller Black Box, about an NTSB analyst investigating a catastrophic plane crash. Script by Adam Gyngell and Fred Fernandez-Armesto.

Agnieszka Smoczyńska (The Lure) has [Hot Spot](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948507/the-lure-directors-ai-thriller-hot-spot-set-for-august-theatrical-release/) arriving August 21 via Focus Features—a dystopian AI thriller hitting theaters against Insidious: Out of the Further.

Louis Leterrier's [The Last House](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948480/first-look-at-louis-leterriers-sci-fi-horror-the-last-house-hitting-netflix-in-august/) (formerly 11817) lands on Netflix August 7. A family gets sealed inside their home in this sci-fi horror piece. First images released.

Damian McCarthy (Caveat, Oddity, Hokum) is [writing a new haunted house film](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948506/hokum-director-damian-mccarthy-writing-a-new-haunted-house-horror-movie/). Details sparse.

Bill Hader makes his feature directorial debut on [They Know](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948521/bill-hader-horror-movie-they-know-adds-2-up-and-comers/), starring himself and Linda Cardellini alongside newcomers Lilou Lang and Lorelei Olivia Mote. A divorced couple's story; plot details thin.

Curry Barker (Obsession) is [tackling a Texas Chainsaw Massacre remake](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948404/curry-barker-promises-brutal-texas-chainsaw-massacre-reimagining-with-heart/). He calls the original "really good for its time," promises something brutal with heart.

The [Sleepaway Camp 1983 screening tour](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948476/felissa-rose-is-taking-sleepaway-camp-on-tour-this-summer/) hits eight cities this summer with Felissa Rose, Peaches Christ, and Michael Varrati. Each night includes drag, live Q&A, and the film on the big screen.
