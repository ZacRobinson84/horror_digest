Giovanni Frezza, who played Bob in Lucio Fulci's *The House by the Cemetery*, [is returning to screen for the first time in over 40 years](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3952561/house-by-the-cemeterys-bob-will-return-in-meta-slasher-run/) in the meta slasher *Run!*

George Eastman, the Italian horror veteran best known for *Antropophagus*, [has died at 83](https://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3952530/italian-horror-actor-and-screenwriter-george-eastman-has-passed-away/). He was also a screenwriter and appeared in Lamberto Bava's *Demons*.

*American Horror Story* Season 13 will [bring back John Carroll Lynch and Mat Fraser](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3952570/twisty-the-clown-returns-american-horror-story-season-13-adds-john-carroll-lynch-mena-suvari-more/) as Twisty the Clown and Paul the Illustrated Seal from *Freak Show*, along with new additions Mena Suvari and Berto Colon.

Lucile Hadžihalilović's *The Ice Tower*, a dark reimagining of "The Snow Queen" starring Marion Cotillard, [arrives on Shudder June 5](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3952511/the-ice-tower-starring-marion-cotillard-heads-to-shudder-in-june/).

Park Chan-Wook's *The Brigands of Rattlecreek* with Matthew McConaughey has [been acquired by Warner Bros.' specialty label Clockwork](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3952429/park-chan-wooks-the-brigands-of-rattlecreek-with-matthew-mcconaughey-acquired-by-warner-bros/) in a mid-teen millions deal. Austin Butler co-stars.

A [director's cut of *The X-Files: I Want to Believe*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3952442/scarier-new-the-x-files-i-want-to-believe-directors-cut-coming-to-disney-in-june/) is coming to Disney+ in June—Chris Carter's original cut before Fox forced him to trim it down for PG-13.

Jenna Ortega is set to star in Leos Carax's surreal dystopian road movie [*Lily May B*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3952457/jenna-ortega-hits-the-road-for-leos-caraxs-dystopian-film-lily-may-b/).
