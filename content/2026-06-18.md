Daveigh Chase, who played Samara in *The Ring*, [has died at 35](https://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3956506/the-ring-actress-daveigh-chase-has-passed-away-at-35/). She died from meningitis and a blood infection.

Netflix has [cancelled *The Boroughs*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3956540/netflix-cancels-the-duffer-brothers-supernatural-mystery-series-the-boroughs/), the Duffer Brothers–produced supernatural mystery series, after one season. The show aired for eight episodes starting in May.

Two new *Evil Dead* films are in motion. [*Evil Dead Wrath*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956490/evil-dead-wrath-is-set-in-1972-and-predates-everything-weve-seen-from-the-franchise/) is set in 1972, predating Raimi's original, and wraps production with a 2028 release planned. A separate film, *Evil Dead Burn*, hits theaters July 10.

Agnieszka Smoczyńska (*The Lure*) directed [*Hot Spot*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956438/hot-spot-trailer-noomi-rapace-stars-as-a-cyber-witch-in-dystopian-sci-fi-thriller-from-the-lure-director/), a dystopian sci-fi thriller starring Noomi Rapace as a cyber witch tangled in a murder mystery. Coming to theaters August 21 via Focus Features.

Mark Jenkin (*Enys Men*) is releasing [*Rose of Nevada*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/interviews/3956396/rose-of-nevada-director-mark-jenkin-on-turning-time-travel-into-a-ghost-story/) in New York and Los Angeles on June 19—a hallucinatory time travel mystery that blurs reality and acts as a ghost story, starring George Mackay and Callum Turner.

Nicolas Winding Refn's [*Her Private Hell*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956428/nicolas-winding-refns-her-private-hell-rated-r-for-bloody-violence-sexual-content/) received its R rating for strong/bloody violence, sexual content, and language. Sophie Thatcher stars. July 24 theatrical release.

Josh Lobo's sophomore feature [*Night After Night*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3956505/night-after-night-poster/) premieres this weekend at Chattanooga Film Festival—a sci-fi thriller with Johnny Sibilly and Alexis Louder.

Spider One's meta-slasher [*Big Baby*](https://bloody-disgusting.com/exclusives/3956300/spider-ones-meta-slasher-big-baby-unleashes-bloody-teaser-ahead-of-august-release/) follows a puppeteer (Brandon Scott) manipulating kills within a nightmare. VOD release August 7.
