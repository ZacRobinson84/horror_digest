[Damian McCarthy](https://rue-morgue.com/exclusive-interview-damian-mccarty-hokum/) on his new film *Hokum*—described as his "most ferocious fright film yet" following *Caveat* and *Oddity*. McCarthy's work lands in that particular register of Irish folk horror that actually has teeth.

Creative Assembly [teased an *Alien: Isolation* sequel](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3948087/creative-assembly-teases-alien-isolation-sequel-reveal/) on Alien Day. Two years into development, details sparse.

*Luna Abyss*, a cosmic horror shooter from Kwalee Labs, [releases May 21](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3948058/cosmic-horror-shooter-luna-abyss-coming-to-pc-consoles-on-may-21-trailer/) on Steam, Epic, PS5, and Xbox Series after a two-year demo run.

Black Lantern Collective [showcased seven horror titles](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3948054/black-lantern-collective-reveals-exorcist-hotline-and-more-trailer/) at Second Wind Games, including *Exorcist Hotline*—a cooperative paranormal investigation game described as "demonic offspring between Phasmophobia and Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes."
