Eli Roth's [Ice Cream Man](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949408/rabid-kids-attack-their-principal-in-eli-roths-ice-cream-man-image/) hits theaters August 7. New still shows principal-on-kid violence.

Sébastien Vaniček (Infested) directs [Evil Dead Burn](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949400/evil-dead-burn-image-possesses-luciane-buchanan-as-a-deadite/), the sixth entry, arriving July 10 with Luciane Buchanan as a Deadite. Details sparse but the Infested pedigree matters.

Nicolas Winding Refn's [Her Private Hell](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949328/nicolas-winding-refns-her-private-hell-set-for-summer-theatrical-release/) gets a July 24 theatrical release via Neon following its Cannes premiere. The film appears positioned as prestige genre fare rather than standard horror.

Álex de la Iglesia (The Day of the Beast, "30 Coins") is [directing Hold the Devil](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949338/davine-joy-randolph-demian-bichir-will-hold-the-devil-for-alex-de-la-iglesia/) with Da'Vine Joy Randolph and Demián Bichir. Joe Barton wrote it; described as blending action with supernatural horror.

[Salem Horror Fest's ninth edition](https://bloody-disgusting.com/editorials/3949269/salem-horror-fest-2026-opening-night-scratches-the-surface-with-dee-wallace-event-report/) opened last night at the Peabody Essex Museum with Dee Wallace in attendance.

Zach Cregger's [Resident Evil](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3949283/zach-creggers-resident-evil-goes-heavy-on-t-virus-mutants-light-on-traditional-zombies/) takes place during RE2's events and emphasizes mutants over traditional zombies, per his trailer breakdown.

[Tales from the Crypt](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3949246/tales-from-the-crypt-now-streaming-for-the-very-first-time-on-shudder/) is finally streaming on Shudder as an exclusive.
