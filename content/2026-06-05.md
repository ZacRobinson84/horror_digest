John Blanche, the artist who defined [Warhammer 40,000's grimdark aesthetic](https://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3954730/legendary-grimdark-warhammer-40000-artist-john-blanche-has-passed-away-at-78/), has died at 78. His work established the visual language for an entire genre hybrid—grimdark sci-fi/fantasy horror—that influenced countless games, books, and films.

[Cannibal Holocaust is returning to theaters](https://rue-morgue.com/cannibal-holocaust-returning-to-theaters-as-youve-never-seen-it-before-new-trailer/) fully uncensored and restored. Big screens, no cuts.

Yeon Sang-ho (Train to Busan) is back with [Colony](https://www.dreadcentral.com/box-office/575707/train-to-busan-directors-colony-rated-r-for-bloody-violent-content/), rated R for bloody violent content. Details sparse but the pedigree alone warrants attention.

Fantasia 2026's [second wave](https://dailydead.com/the-fantasia-international-film-festival-announces-a-stacked-second-wave-of-titles-for-its-30th-edition/) includes Jane Schoenbrun's Teenage Sex and Death at Camp Miasma, Cape Fear's series finale, and work from Larry Fessenden and The Adams Family. The festival runs July 16–August 2 in Montréal.

Henry Chaisson (Antlers, Servant) made his feature directorial debut with [Recluse](https://dailydead.com/tribeca-2026-review-recluse-is-effective-thrilling-gothic-horror/), a sound-driven gothic horror that premiered at Tribeca. An audio engineer returns home to New England after her father's accident and finds something wrong in the house.

[Fall 2: Deadpoint](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3954657/fall-2-deadpoint-official-trailer-franchise-heads-to-thailand-for-fear-of-heights-sequel/) takes the acrophobia concept to Thailand with new characters. Hits theaters September 2.

Eli Roth's [Ice Cream Man](https://ihorror.com/eli-roths-ice-cream-man-red-band-trailer-just-made-ice-cream-trucks-terrifying-forever/) red band trailer dropped this week. A mysterious vendor rolls into a seaside town; the trailer suggests body horror and practical effects.
