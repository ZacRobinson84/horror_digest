Kevin Bacon is reteaming with Jeremy Slater (Mortal Kombat II, "The Exorcist" TV) for [Summoner](https://www.fangoria.com/kevin-bacon-summoner/), a demonic horror feature from Amazon MGM Studios. Slater makes his directorial debut; details are sparse.

A new [Blair Witch Project](https://www.fangoria.com/the-blair-witch-project-remake-official-release-date/) film is coming September 24, 2027 via Lionsgate and Blumhouse-Atomic Monster. Dylan Clark (Portrait of God) is producing. No other details yet.

[What We Do in the Shadows](https://www.dreadcentral.com/news/577939/what-we-do-in-the-shadows-one-of-horrors-most-underrated-movies-is-getting-a-sequel/) is getting a sequel. The Taika Waititi–directed vampire mockumentary spawned a long-running TV series, but a film follow-up had been dormant until now.

Hideo Kojima's [OD](https://bloody-disgusting.com/video-games/3957169/hideo-kojima-teases-revolutionary-new-gameplay-system-for-horror-in-od/), the horror game collaboration with Jordan Peele, will use a "fear-based" mechanic Kojima remains cagey about. Expect the usual cryptic Kojima messaging.

Night School Studio (Oxenfree) is bringing [Unhinged](https://www.dreadcentral.com/gaming/577867/netflix-to-get-unhinged-with-immersive-horror-video-game-starring-zoe-kravitz-sadie-sink-and-troy-baker/) to Netflix on June 30—a play-on-TV thriller starring Zoë Kravitz, Sadie Sink, and Troy Baker.

John Carpenter will appear at Midsummer Scream (August 7–9, Long Beach) to celebrate [Cathedral](https://dailydead.com/horror-highlights-cathedral-the-swamp-horror-horror-trivia-app/), his landmark debut graphic novel releasing simultaneously with a companion album.
