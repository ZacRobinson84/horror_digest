Gerry Conway, co-creator of The Punisher and Werewolf by Night, [has died at 73](https://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3948331/the-punisher-werewolf-by-night-creator-gerry-conway-has-passed-away/). Conway shaped Marvel's horror output through the '70s and beyond, establishing the Punisher's tragic origin in Marvel Preview #2 and co-creating numerous supernatural characters that defined the publisher's darker work.

Mariclare Costello, who played the lake-dwelling vampire Emily in the 1971 cult film [Let's Scare Jessica to Death](https://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3948259/lets-scare-jessica-to-death-actress-mariclare-costello-has-passed-away/), has passed away at 90. That underwater emergence remains one of early '70s horror's most arresting images.

Park Chan-Wook's long-gestating [The Brigands of Rattlecreek](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948268/park-chan-wooks-the-brigands-of-rattlecreek-to-star-matthew-mcconaughey-austin-butler-pedro-pascal/) is finally moving forward as his next film, with Matthew McConaughey, Austin Butler, Pedro Pascal, and Tang Wei attached. A Western revenge thriller, it's been in development for over a decade.

Zach Cregger's [Resident Evil adaptation](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948239/new-website-for-zach-creggers-resident-evil-reveals-the-official-logo/) is hitting theaters this September. Sony launched the official site with a logo reveal; full marketing machinery is about to engage.

Sophie Thatcher (Heretic), Erin Kellyman (28 Years Later), and Joe Alwyn star in [Cavendish](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948252/sophie-thatcher-erin-kellyman-joe-alywn-to-star-in-witch-hunt-thriller-cavendish/), Christopher Andrews' witch hunt thriller set in 17th-century Britain. It's being pitched as visceral action layered with sharp, unexpected humor.

A feature-length Crow fan film, [Days of Sodom](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948236/days-of-sodom-watch-the-crow-feature-length-fan-film-now/), is now streaming free. Cody Faulk's 160-minute supernatural revenge noir may appeal to those left cold by last year's remake.

Korean YA horror series [If Wishes Could Kill](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3948180/korean-horror-series-if-wishes-could-kill-is-now-streaming-on-netflix/) is now on Netflix. A wish-granting app called Girigo demands fatal prices; the show blends school-life melodrama with occult machinery.

Behind the Mask: The Rise of Leslie Vernon is [finally getting a sequel](https://bloody-disgusting.com/interviews/3948212/why-now-is-the-right-time-for-sequel-behind-the-mask-ii-the-return-of-leslie-vernon/). Behind the Mask II: The Return of Leslie Vernon is in active development, decades after the indie meta-slasher cult favorite.
