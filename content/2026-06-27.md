[Gary Dauberman is writing Five Nights at Freddy's 3](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3957670/five-nights-at-freddys-3-update-gary-dauberman-writing-the-script/), bringing IT and Salem's Lot credentials to the animatronic franchise. No director attached yet.

[Kiah Roache-Turner (Wyrmwood, Beast of War) is shooting Lady Death](https://bloody-disgusting.com/exclusives/3957603/lady-death-exclusive-first-look-kiah-roache-turners-new-world-war-ii-action-horror-movie/), a WWII action-horror in production outside Adelaide. Details sparse, but the heightened tone suggests this isn't straightforward period warfare.

[Los Vampires premieres at Fantasia](https://bloody-disgusting.com/exclusives/3957440/los-vampires-trailer/) (July 16–Aug 2). A period horror built around the production of the 1931 Spanish Dracula—murder on a haunted set. Actual period intrigue rather than just another vampire retread.

[Suzanne Desrocher-Romero, founder and president of the George A. Romero Foundation, has died](https://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3957555/suzanne-desrocher-romero-has-passed-away/) of natural causes. She stewarded Romero's legacy and the foundation's archival work.

[WB Animation is developing an adult animated Dark Shadows series](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3957525/dark-shadows-wb-animation-announces-adult-animated-series-based-on-1960s-tv-show/). No creative team announced yet beyond the studio greenlight.

[Lee Cronin's The Mummy hits HBO Max on July 3](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3957615/lee-cronins-the-mummy-makes-streaming-debut-next-week/), with a linear premiere on HBO the next day.

[Ice Nine Kills released "Play Dead" for Dead by Daylight](https://bloody-disgusting.com/music/3957556/play-dead-watch-the-official-dead-by-daylight-music-video-from-ice-nine-kills/), a tie-in track with a music video featuring Tony Hawk, Devon Sawa, and Krsy Fox.
