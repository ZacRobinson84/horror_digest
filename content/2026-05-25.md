Curry Barker's [*Obsession* pulled off a rare box office feat](https://www.dreadcentral.com/box-office/573880/obsession-just-pulled-off-one-of-horrors-rarest-box-office-feats/), earning 30% more in its second weekend—the kind of hold that almost never happens in horror. Barker is [teasing a director's cut](https://www.dreadcentral.com/streaming-home-video/573871/curry-barker-teases-obsession-directors-cut-will-the-nc-17-head-smash-make-the-cut/) that may restore an NC-17 scene that got trimmed for theatrical.

*Terror: Endless Night*, a [turn-based survival strategy game](https://bloody-disgusting.com/?p=3953105) inspired by true events, dropped a new trailer four years after announcement. Details sparse on what actually differentiates it beyond arctic setting and "merciless decisions."

Jason Voorhees [joins *Dead by Daylight*](https://www.dreadcentral.com/gaming/573864/friday-the-13th-icon-jason-voorhees-officially-joins-dead-by-daylight-this-june/) on June 16. Unsurprising licensing move; *Friday the 13th* IP has been circulating through multiplayer games for years now.

AMC is launching [*The Vampire Lestat: After Dark*](https://ihorror.com/the-vampire-lestat-after-dark-gives-fans-a-front-row-seat-to-season-3-chaos/), a half-hour companion show where cast and creators unpack season 3 episodes in real time. The *Interview with the Vampire* universe continuing its media saturation play.
