Magnus von Horn's English-language debut [The Passenger](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948852/jeremy-strong-to-star-in-historical-escape-thriller-the-passenger-from-the-girl-with-the-needle-director/) stars Jeremy Strong in a historical escape thriller adapted from a 1938 German novel. Von Horn co-wrote with Aleko Gotscheff; details sparse on the particulars.

Taka Ichise, producer on Ringu and Ju-on, [has signed a development and financing deal](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948807/the-ring-the-grudge-producer-taka-ichise-developing-horror-films-with-the-life-of-chuck-outfit/) with QWGmire (The Life of Chuck, Normal) for a slate of four new horror films targeting the US market.

Shudder picked up [Hallowarrior](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948768/shudder-acquires-post-apocalyptic-thriller-hallowarrior-starring-milly-shapiro/) ahead of its Tribeca premiere on June 10. Milly Shapiro plays the last human on earth after a plague, scavenging a wasteland; the film lands on the streamer this October.

The Horror Collective acquired [Fresh Meat](https://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3948809/influencer-horror-movie-fresh-meat-getting-a-release-from-the-horror-collective/), an influencer-centered horror film from Daniel Turres, with international sales launching at Cannes Film Market.

Blumhouse Television is [adapting Shusuke Shizukui's Japanese psychological novel Sparks](https://bloody-disgusting.com/tv/3948674/blumhouse-to-adapt-japanese-psychological-thriller-novel-sparks-for-tv/) for TV, with Krystal Houghton Ziv (The Purge, CSI: Miami) writing and executive producing the pilot.

Damian Mc Carty's [Hokum](https://rue-morgue.com/movie-review-hokum-masterfully-prints-the-witch-legend-with-a-cackle/) is a witch legend film that delivers composed scares and humor in equal measure, per Rue Morgue's review.
